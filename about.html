About Us

Welcome to Filco Diamond Tools

Welcome to Filco Diamond Tools, your trusted partner in precision drilling solutions. Since our founding in 2018, we have dedicated ourselves to redefining quality in the diamond core bit industry. Through advanced powder metallurgy and rigorous R&D, we've evolved into a leading manufacturer, setting new standards in the field.

The Filco Advantage

What sets Filco apart is our unwavering commitment to quality and innovation. We leverage state-of-the-art German machinery and stringent quality control processes to ensure every product meets the highest standards. Our expertise allows us to offer tailored solutions designed for drilling through various types of stones, ensuring you have the perfect tool for any application.

We believe in transparency and customer confidence. That's why we invite you to visit our Experience Center, where you can trial our products firsthand. Whether you're currently using another brand or exploring new options, you're welcome to compare our tools side-by-side with any competitor's. We're confident you'll experience the difference that makes Filco a preferred choice.

In short, Filco Diamond Tools is more than just a manufacturer. We are a partner dedicated to providing you with reliable, high-quality drilling solutions and a customer experience that stands out in the industry.

Why Choose Us?

The Filco Difference

At Filco Diamond Tools, we don't just supply tools—we build your brand. As your trusted OEM and wholesale manufacturing partner, we focus on empowering your business rather than competing with it. Here's what makes the Filco difference:

  • Market Protection: We exclusively serve large importers and wholesalers, ensuring that your market position is always protected.
  • Industrial Scale: Our production capacity exceeds 120,000 units per month, scaling effortlessly with your needs.
  • Tailor-Made Solutions: We design customized bonds for specific stone applications, ensuring your tools are perfectly suited to your requirements.
  • Dry Drilling Specialists: Our tools are engineered for dry and sandwich drilling, providing maximum durability and speed without the need for water.
  • Rigorous Quality Checks: We inspect every single piece in our production unit individually. This meticulous attention reduces our rejection rate to less than 1%, compared to the industry norm of 7-10%.
  • Consistent Quality: Our zero-defect policy ensures every batch is identical and top quality.
  • Direct Factory Advantage: Benefit from faster turnaround times and superior quality control compared to imports.

In short, Filco is committed to delivering exceptional tools with unmatched quality and a seamless partnership experience to help your brand thrive.
