A Message From Our Founder

From Corporate Strategy to Industrial Innovation

After completing my MBA and gaining organizational experience with one of the world's leading multi-national corporations (MNCs), I saw a gap in the industrial tool market for a manufacturer that combined corporate precision with deep technical research.

In 2017, I started Filco Diamond Tools with a modest production capacity of 4,000 units per month. My goal was to move beyond generic imports and master the science of powder metallurgy. Through extensive R&D, we developed a bond that enables end-users to drill dry and super-fast in any stone—from the hardest granites to abrasive quartz.

"Today, that vision has scaled into an industrial powerhouse, producing 150,000 core bits per month. We aren't just making tools; we are engineering efficiency for the global market."

— Rupinder Singh
Managing Director, Filco Diamond Tools

Technical Excellence

Proprietary R&D

We protect our partners' market position by refusing to compromise on the science of the segment.

01

High-Density Metallic Matrix

Our segments feature a scientifically balanced foundation that ensures a controlled erosion rate, maximizing tool life and cutting consistency.

02

Thermal Stability

Advanced additives dissipate heat rapidly, preventing "glazing" during dry-drilling and ensuring consistent performance even under extreme conditions.

Superior segment-to-body brazing that exceeds international safety standards, ensuring structural integrity under high-stress applications.
